Building the Future of
MSME Credit

We believe no creditworthy MSME should be denied a loan because of poor documentation or lack of financial expertise.

India's 63 million MSMEs form the backbone of the economy — yet most struggle to access formal credit. The problem isn't creditworthiness. It's preparation. CreditGuide AI exists to close that gap, empowering businesses with AI-powered tools that were previously only available to large corporates with dedicated finance teams.
